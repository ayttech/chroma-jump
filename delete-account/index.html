Delete Account & Data

No Account Required

Chroma Jump does not require user accounts or registration. There is no account to delete.

Delete Local Game Data

All game data (high scores, settings) is stored locally on your device. To delete all data:

Android

  1. Go to Settings → Apps → Chroma Jump
  2. Tap Storage & cache
  3. Tap Clear data and Clear cache

Alternatively, uninstalling the app will remove all associated data.

iOS

  1. Press and hold the Chroma Jump icon on your home screen
  2. Tap Remove App → Delete App
  3. Reinstall from the App Store if desired

Third-Party Data

Google AdMob may have collected anonymized advertising data. To manage this:

  1. Visit Google Data & Privacy settings
  2. Review and manage your ad personalization preferences
  3. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings
