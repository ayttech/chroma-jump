Support

We're here to help you with any issues or questions about Chroma Jump.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I play?
    Tap to jump upward. Match your ball's color with the obstacle segment to pass through safely. Collect stars for points.
  • How do color switchers work?
    When you pass through a color switcher (the small colorful circle between obstacles), your ball changes to a random color.
  • How does the level system work?
    Every 50 points you level up. Higher levels mean faster obstacles and new obstacle types.
  • What does "Watch Ad to Continue" do?
    After a game over, you can watch a short video ad to revive and continue from where you left off.
  • How do I reset my high score?
    Uninstall and reinstall the app to reset all saved data.

Troubleshooting

  • App crashes on startup
    Make sure your device is running Android 7.0+ or iOS 13+. Try restarting the app.
  • Ads not loading
    Ensure you have an active internet connection. Ads may take a moment to load.
  • No sound
    Check that your device volume is turned up and not on silent mode.
